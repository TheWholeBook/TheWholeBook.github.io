Haikus and Poems

Written by: Kaedehara Kazuha

Introduction

Come driving rain or winds that churn,
I shall return, by blade alone,
Armed, if barefoot, to my home...

Traveler's Birthday

Sun and moon rejoice
Birds of dawn sing songs anew
Far from home, with you.

In Demo

Solitary cloud,
Shadow in the setting sun,
Stirs the drifter's heart.

His Musings

If one's heart is empty,
All under heaven is empty.
But if one's heart is pure
All under heaven is pure.
